Calmdoku Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Calmdoku does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate a server for this app, use third-party analytics, use advertising SDKs, or track users across apps or websites.

If you delete the app, locally stored app data may be removed from your device according to iOS behavior.
